Indicator Rubric (1-4)

Quick Reference

Visible Scholar Thinking

  • 1: Minimal notebooks; thinking mostly hidden.
  • 2: Some notes; reasoning is partial.
  • 3: Consistent notes; thinking is visible.
  • 4: Rich annotations; students explain reasoning.

Problem Solving Mastery

  • 1: Little strategy use; high confusion.
  • 2: Emerging strategies; inconsistent success.
  • 3: Clear strategy use; solid decoding.
  • 4: Strategic mastery; students adapt approaches.

Goal Agency

  • 1: Goals unclear; students passive.
  • 2: Goals mentioned; limited ownership.
  • 3: Students track goals and progress.
  • 4: Students lead data talks and next steps.
